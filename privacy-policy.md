# Privacy Policy for Clara Chat

Effective date: 2026-02-15

Clara Chat ("we", "us", "our") provides a WhatsApp-based conversational assistant.
This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1) Data We Collect

When you use Clara Chat, we may collect and store:

- Your WhatsApp phone number
- Messages you send to Clara Chat
- Replies generated by Clara Chat
- Basic profile preferences (for example language, corrections mode)
- Technical metadata needed for service operation (timestamps, usage counters)

## 2) How We Use Data

We use data to:

- Provide and operate the chat service
- Generate responses to your messages
- Apply your preferences across conversations
- Improve reliability, security, and abuse prevention

## 3) Legal Basis

Where applicable, we process data based on:

- Your request to use the service
- Our legitimate interest in operating and securing Clara Chat
- Compliance with applicable legal obligations

## 4) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be processed by service providers strictly to operate Clara Chat, including:

- Meta (WhatsApp Cloud API)
- Cloud infrastructure providers used to run the application
- AI model providers used to generate responses

## 5) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to operate the service and for security/operational purposes.
You can request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

## 6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data.
No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7) Your Rights and Deletion

You can delete your account and conversation data directly by sending:

`!deleteaccount`

This permanently removes your account profile and stored message history from Clara Chat systems.

You can also reset your chat experience without deleting your account by sending:

`!reset`

## 8) International Processing

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on our infrastructure providers.

## 9) Children's Privacy

Clara Chat is not intended for children under 13 (or higher minimum age required by local law).

## 10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above.
